# Privacy Policy: FCDex Economy

**Last Updated:** March 31, 2026

### 1. Data Collection
To facilitate the economy system, we store the following:
* **Discord User ID:** To associate "Pounds" balances and game statistics with your account.
* **Transaction Logs:** A history of `!pay` and `!rob` actions to prevent fraud and assist in support tickets.

### 2. Data Usage
* Data is used solely for calculating leaderboard rankings and processing game outcomes.
* We do not track message content beyond the commands required to play.

### 3. Data Storage
* All financial data is stored securely using **LibSQL**.
* Sensitive bot credentials (tokens) are managed via `.env` and are never exposed to the public.

### 4. Data Deletion
* If you leave the FCDex server, your economy data may be archived. You can request a manual wipe of your "Pounds" history by contacting the **TheFCProject** staff.
